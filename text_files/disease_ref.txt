## Diabetes Prediction
Dataset:
- Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/pima-indians-diabetes-database

Algorithms Commonly Used:
- Logistic Regression
- Random Forest
- Support Vector Machines
- Neural Networks

Relevant Articles:
1. Diabetes Prediction Using Machine Learning (Towards Data Science):
https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-024-78519-8
2. Predicting Diabetes with Python:
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/diabetes-prediction-machine-learning-project-using-python-streamlit/
